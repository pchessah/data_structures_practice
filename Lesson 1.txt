- Adding data to the beiginning of an array is more difficult than to the end of an array.
- Both push() and unshift() mutate the array.


- pop() and shift() are also mutator fucntions for removing items from an array.


- splice(indexStart, 0, elements) ===> Adds elements in the middle of an array starting at the specified index.
- splice(indexStart, 2) ==>Removes 2 elements starting from the specified indexStart position.
